3. Children’s Privacy

Shadow Browser does not knowingly allow individuals under the age of 13 to use our services.

  • Prohibited Use: If you are under 13, please do not use Shadow Browser.
  • Actions Taken: If we are alerted that a user under 13 has used our service, we will take immediate steps to remove any information related to that user from our systems.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We implement the following measures to safeguard your privacy:

  • No Data Logging: Shadow Browser does not log or store browsing history, IP addresses, or personal information on our servers.
  • Encrypted Communication: All data transmitted through Shadow Browser is encrypted for security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Shadow Browser is designed to prioritize your privacy:
